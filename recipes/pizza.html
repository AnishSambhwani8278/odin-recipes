Veg Pizza

Description

This classic vegetarian pizza recipe features a homemade dough topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and a variety of delicious vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Vegetables (such as bell peppers, mushrooms, onions, olives, spinach)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough and transfer it to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Add your favorite vegetables as toppings.
  6. Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  7. Remove from the oven, slice, and serve hot.
