Veggie Burger

Description

This vegetarian burger recipe features flavorful veggie patties made from ingredients like black beans, mushrooms, and spices, served on toasted burger buns with fresh toppings.

Ingredients

  • Black beans
  • Mushrooms
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Mash black beans in a bowl and mix with finely chopped mushrooms, breadcrumbs, diced onion, and spices.
  3. Form the mixture into patties and cook for 3-4 minutes per side, or until browned and heated through.
  4. Toast the burger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  5. Assemble the burgers by placing the cooked patties on the bottom bun.
  6. Add lettuce, tomato, onion, avocado, and any other desired toppings.
  7. Top with the remaining bun and serve hot.
