Pasta

Description

This simple pasta recipe features al dente spaghetti tossed in a rich tomato sauce and topped with grated Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Tomato sauce
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add spaghetti to the boiling water and cook according to package instructions until al dente.
  3. Heat tomato sauce in a separate saucepan until warmed through.
  4. Drain the cooked spaghetti and return it to the pot.
  5. Pour the warm tomato sauce over the spaghetti and toss to coat evenly.
  6. Divide the pasta among serving plates and sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
