Garlic Bread

Description

This delicious garlic bread recipe features crispy bread slices brushed with garlic-infused butter and sprinkled with parsley.

Ingredients

  • French bread
  • Butter
  • Garlic cloves

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan and add minced garlic.
  3. Cut the French bread into slices and place them on a baking sheet.
  4. Brush the bread slices with the garlic butter mixture.
  5. Sprinkle chopped parsley over the bread slices.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the bread is golden brown and crispy.
  7. Remove from the oven and serve hot.
